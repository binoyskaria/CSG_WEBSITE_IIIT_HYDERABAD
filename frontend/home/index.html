About CSG

The Computer Systems Group undertakes research and development in all fundamental aspects of Computing Systems, spanning across hardware, software, and theory. Our dynamic team focuses on innovation and excellence in each of these domains, aiming to push the boundaries of technology and knowledge.

Our Vision

CSG envisions being a leader in computing, bridging areas like systems security and cloud computing with innovative research and practical applications.

Deeply committed to fostering robust industry-academia collaborations, we are dedicated to nurturing the next generation of technology leaders and driving impactful, transformative technological advancements with a global reach and societal impact.
